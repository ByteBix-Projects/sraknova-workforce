Frequently Asked Questions

What registration details verify Sraknova's legitimacy?

What is the training and vetting process for security guards?
Every recruit undergoes a thorough identity verification check (including Aadhaar verification and local police verification references). Selected guards are trained in gate access control, physical security drills, emergency response, fire extinguisher handling, and customer relations.
Are there statutory compliance provisions for deployed guards?
Yes. Sraknova operates under strict statutory labor compliance in India. Deployed guards are registered under Employee Provident Fund (EPF), Employee State Insurance (ESI), and are covered under standard liability insurance (workmen's compensation) to protect clients from operational liabilities.
How quickly can guards be mobilised to my site?
For standard sites (offices, schools, residential societies in Lohardaga/Ranchi) requiring 2-5 guards, we can typically deploy within 48 to 72 hours of agreement signing. For larger industrial contracts requiring shifts, mobilization takes 5-10 business days for logistics and site-specific training.
Do you support specialized VIP or armed security services?
Yes, we can provide elite supervisor guards and armed personal guards (subject to valid arm licenses and necessary regional permissions). These services require customized contract layouts; please get in touch via our intake form for details.
